About Us

The Xfactor Organization is dedicated to fighting the drug abuse pandemic among young Nigerians. We provide opportunities for victims of drugs and substance abuse to escape the scourge and to transform their lives. Our Drug Awareness campaigns, Exit Bootcamps and the X program has helped thousands of young Nigerians to stay drugs free.
Drugs kill!!!

The Xfactor Organization's "X program!" is helping young people reclaim their lives.

The Numbers That Count!

The Xfactor Organization has rescued 4,315 young people from substance abuse since 2018.
